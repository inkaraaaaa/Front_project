Sport Club

Comprehensive and affordable personal development through physical exercise. Club conducts football training games, basketball, tennis, etc.

Orleans

Women's dance club. At the moment the club has three directions dance: folk dances, modern dances and K-POP dances. In Week 2 sessions of 2 hours per day every direction. Trainings are held after training sessions. AT every semester the club organizes team building for club members.

Music Club

The club has been in existence for over 15 years. Main activities are performances at various events of the university and city level.

Dombyra Club

Is the first club in the SDU to deal with development of Kazakh culture among young people. Club members perform folk kui and contemporary songs at various events.

Vision Club

Club for self-realization of girls. Here you learn how to cook, organize concerts, weave, draw, help those in need and much more.

Shapagat

One of the oldest SDU clubs that does charity work. Club members do not divide society into classes and organize various kinds of events in order to give warmth and do good.

The League of Volunteers

An organization that does charity work. We take part in various socially significant campaigns: dissemination of the necessary information, preparation and holding of public actions.

Panem

Debate in Russian. One of the three debate leagues in SDU. As an educational club, we are engaged in the development of the intellectual abilities of students.

Qazaq Debate Club

Debate in Kazakh. A club with a rich history. Included in the top 3 debate clubs in Kazakhstan. To date, the club has more than 150 cups and prizes.

International Debate Club

Debate in English. It is aimed at developing students' ability to constructively speak, speak and defend their point of view, as well as popularize the English language.

PandUp

An organization that specializes in videography. Members of the club shoot various commercials, videos, short films and develop themselves in the fields of the film industry and directing.

Zhasa Club

Engaged in the development of creative skills among students in the following areas: Photography, Videography, Graphic Design.

Academic Club

Assistance to students of various faculties in subjects. Organize additional classes and provide legal support.

Art Club

Community of the most creative university students. The aim of the club is to bring together people interested in drawing, decorative arts and needlework and give them the opportunity to develop their talents. The Art Club hosts creative evenings on academic drawing, lettering, painting and book lovers evenings.

Event Club

A student club that organizes various kinds of events inside and outside the university.

Mountain Kings

A club that organizes trips to nature, mainly to the mountains.

King's Speech

A team of future leaders. Our goal is to help students overcome their fears and discover their strengths. Working in a friendly team, we reach new heights.

IT Club

The club consists of several groups moving in different directions of the IT sphere. Our platform will help students to realize themselves in the IT field. We share experience, knowledge among ourselves, create teams and contribute to the development of the IT industry.

Red Crescent

The mission of the club is to raise the level of awareness of the population about the knowledge of first aid according to the standards of the International Committee of the Red Cross and the constant development of methods for conducting trainings in accordance with the criteria of the above-mentioned committee.

Enactus

Implements socio-economic projects for 17 UN goals. It is an international non-profit organization that brings together active students, academic and business leaders.

UNESCO Club

The club organizes online and offline events according to the MUN model and represents SDU at the republican level.

Book Club

The club strives to teach as many people as possible to read books on an ongoing basis, to be able to freely and clearly express their thoughts. Periodically organizes online/offline meetings dedicated to any topic.
